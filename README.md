Task:
Make a Dog Years converter

For dog years converter was used this formula:
You need to multiply the natural logarithm of the dog's age by 16 and add 31. 
This formula gives a very accurate match for the age of a person and a dog.

or cat years converter was used this formula:
The first year of a cat's life is equal to the whole 15 years of a person's life. T
he second is equal to 24 years of life, and every subsequent year you simply add 4 years of life.
